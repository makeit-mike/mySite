The very first picture on the World Wide Web was a GIF, so it is good to know where we stand today with its popularity.

The GIF today is a world standard, being used in almost every popular website, especially this one.

In terms of how the GIF has changed today, one major breakthrough has been made.

Before, the GIF could only play a series of pictures, to make a short video, but now we can synchronise sound with our GIFs to sound.

In essence, the GIF is now able to be a short video, with audio playback, and while they are annoying when they autoplay, they are still an evolution.
