Origin of the GIF





Image Map for Site

The GIF, less commonly known as the Graphics Interchange Format, was created in 1987.

The creators name was Steve Wilhite, and he was a developer for CompuServe.

Much like with most things developers deal with, the idea was to save space.

A cool little known fact, is that the GIF was created before the World Wide Web was!
